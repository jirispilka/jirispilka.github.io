Jiří Spilka.

Research and Development Engineer at Concerto.ai

I’m currently working as a research and development engineer at Concerto.ai. Here, I've been part of a team developing conversational AI capable of handling question answering from both structured and unstructured data, scheduling appointments, and more.

I hold a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence and Biocybernetics from the Czech Technical University in Prague. My research there mainly focused on signal processing and machine learning, particularly in analyzing fetal heart rate. I worked as a researcher at the Czech Institute of Informatics, Robotics, and Cybernetics. I also had the pleasure of working with Patrice Abry during my post-doc at the Physics Department, ENS Lyon, France. Prior to that, I served as a Research and Development Engineer in the Cardiology Department at BTL Medical Technologies CZ, implementing signal processing and classification algorithms for Holter and resting ECGs.

I greatly enjoyed teaching an Artificial Intelligence course at the Department of Cybernetics, Faculty of Electrical Engineering, alongside Petr Posik and Radek Marik.

If you are interested in working with me on academic or industrial signal processing and/or machine learning projects feel free to contact me about available topics.
